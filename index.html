About Me

Hello, my name is Joshua Champagne and I am a former teacher turned software developer. I'm learning full-stack web development through university courses, online guided coded courses, and practicing coding everyday. I am a veracious learner and passionate about bringing ideas to life through various web technologies and frameworks.


I'm comfortable with responsive HTML5, CSS3, and front-end tools like Javascript, PHP, Boostrap and Wordpress. I've also worked with backend services utilizing Python and mySQL. I'm currently working on putting together a portfolio of small projects and am building a large personal project in the Django CMS.


I have additional experience in the Information Technology field serving as a Level 1 Help Desk Technician for 3 years. While in this role I helped update and maintain hundreds of production machines as well as maintain and update production code for various projects in a number of languages including Perl, Lisp, and Python. I also updated and managed internal documentation pages using HTML and CSS.
